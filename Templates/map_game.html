Map Game!

By, Spencer Evans-Cole

The goal of this game is to have the oxygen level be above 80% by the end of the year. To do this there are 5 events you can use at the bottom of the screen to prevent disasters. Disaster periodicly happen. Whenever you do enough damage to dystroy an event the marker will turn black. On the side of the screen it shows your current oxygen level, total active events, and the total ammount of health of all active events. The top of the screen tells you the most recent event, where it is and how much health and damage it has. The left side of the screen shows a counter of time that has passed. Good luck!
